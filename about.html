Bike Chain Length Calculator

Version 1.03b



As the driving force of the bike, your chain sees a lot of wear and tear. Over time, distance, and cycling conditions, the rivets and rollers begin to wear out, causing the chain to actually stretch in length. A brand new chain will measure 12 inches across 12 links, so if you measure your chain and it is 12 1/16 inches across 12 links then it's time to replace it. If it is stretched even further, it has most likely damaged your cogs. The first thing you're going to need to know is the necessary length for your new chain. There are a few methods to accomplish this, which we will break down for you in this article.
